SentiX

make global change work for you

Multi-source satellite time-series remote sensing analysis platform, quantifying, visualizing and monitoring land surface dynamics across the globe, turning raw satellite imagery into actionable change insights.

Platform Core Capabilities

多源对地观测卫星 · 地表时序变化智能解析平台

Multi-Temporal Change Detection

对比不同时期卫星影像，自动识别植被消长、水域变迁、建设用地扩张、滑坡及土地荒漠化等地表渐变与突变现象，量化变化幅度与关键时间节点。

Multi-Source Satellite Data Processing

兼容光学、雷达等各类主流卫星遥感数据集，统一完成大气校正、云量剔除、波段运算、植被水体建筑指数（NDVI、NDWI、NDBI）标准化预处理流程。

Spatial Visualization Output

生成变化分级专题图、差值热力图、时序统计折线图表，直观展现区域长期演化规律，分析成果可导出，支撑科研研究、国土规划、生态环境监测复盘工作。

Custom Region Analysis

支持自定义划定分析范围，面向城市扩张、湿地演变、生态修复、林木采伐、洪涝灾害评估等场景，开展定向批量地表变化监测分析。

Our Mission

Turn global satellite observation data into understandable, usable decision support

Global land surface is constantly reshaped by human activities and natural forces. SentiX lowers the technical threshold for remote sensing data application, enabling researchers, planners and environmental practitioners to track long-term land evolution conveniently, so that global change truly works for your research and practice.
